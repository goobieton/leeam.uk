Discord Bot

7 / 12 / 2023

Making a discord bot *was* worth it for me at the time. Being able to listen to music together while in vc was useful. The only problem was I had no clue on how to use Discord.js, so i had to learn it.

After learning the basic fundemantals for JavaScript, I had to spend a long time reading the docs, and making a suitable base for the bot.

Then, even after that, I had to implament lavalink to get music sources from different places, (Youtube, Soundcloud, Spotify ect) but it is just nice to have.

But overall it was a nice project, but it has no use to me anymore.
